About

I find excitement in planning for my next creative project, whether it's a storyline, animation, drawing, or a piece of music. When I need a break or am feeling lazy, I enjoy reading novels or writing, and playing games in my free time. Looking back on my past projects, I often think, "What was I thinking when I made this?" As reflected in my work, I gravitate towards creating non-photorealistic renderings. My dream is to bring joy to others through my creative endeavors, inspired by the joy I've experienced from other artists.

I predominantly use Blender for various tasks such as shading, lighting, texturing, modeling, rigging, animation, and video editing. Additionally, I turn to Photoshop for creating textures and sketches, Maya for sculpting, and Audacity for sound editing and enhancements.

If I sound like the kind of person that would fit your team, take a peek at my CV. I’m excited about the idea of teaming up with you all and looking forward to the chance to work together!
